Biography

Dr Sumit Kunwar is an avid clinical researcher with a keen interest in systematic reviews and meta-analysis. He has published extensively in medical journals..

Dr Sumit Kunwar is board certified in internal medicine and rheumatology.

He joined us after completing his fellowship program at the Georgetown University Washington Hospital Center program. Prior to his rheumatology fellowship Dr Kunwar was an internist at the Lakes Region General Hospital in Laconia, NH where he was also a clinical assistant professor in Internal Medicine at the University of New England.

 He is an avid clinical researcher with a keen interest in systematic reviews and meta-analysis. He has published extensively in medical journals.

Dr. Kunwar sees patients at our Bel Air office.
