What We donâ€™t Treat

Rheumatologists do not typically manage chronic pain, chronic Lyme, long Covid, Ehlers-Danlos syndrome, hypermobility syndromes, chronic fatigue syndrome (myalgic encephalomyelitis), or non-inflammatory spine problems (degenerative arthritis of the spine, spinal stenosis, degenerative disc disease). We are able to help establish the diagnosis of fibromyalgia, but do not manage it long term, so we can guide you to others who can best provide ongoing care. Our values

Our Values

Quality

Our rheumatologists undergo extensive training in both Internal Medicine and Rheumatology, ensuring specialized care. We prioritize evidence-based medicine, focusing on proven treatments rather than personal opinion, to deliver the highest standard of care for every patient.

Access

We understand that timely access to a rheumatologist is crucial. That's why we strive to schedule new patients as soon as possible, ensuring you're seen without unnecessary delays. If urgent, we aim to accommodate you within 48 hours, even if it means staying late to provide the care you need.

Waiting Time

We value your time as much as our own. To minimize waiting, we ask you to arrive promptly for your appointment. New patients should arrive 15 minutes early to complete paperwork, ensuring you are seen on time and we can stay on schedule for all our patients.
